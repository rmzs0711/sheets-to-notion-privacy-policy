Privacy Policy

Sheet to Notion - Chrome Extension

Last updated: January 12, 2026

Overview

Sheet to Notion is a Chrome extension that allows you to export data from Google Sheets to Notion pages. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

Key Point: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. All data processing happens locally in your browser.

Data We Access

To provide its functionality, the extension accesses the following data:

  • Google Sheets Data: When you choose to export a spreadsheet, the extension reads the content of that specific sheet. This data is only used to create the corresponding content in Notion and is never stored or transmitted elsewhere.
  • Google Account Information: We use Google OAuth to authenticate you and obtain read-only access to your spreadsheets. We only request the minimum permission needed (spreadsheets.readonly).
  • Notion API Token: You provide your Notion integration token to enable the extension to create pages in your Notion workspace. This token is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API.

Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser:

  • Notion API Token: Stored securely using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API to persist your connection to Notion.
  • Selected Notion Page ID: Stored locally to remember your preferred destination page.
  • Export Preferences: Your chosen export mode (Table or Database) is stored locally.

This data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to any external servers owned by us.

Third-Party Services

The extension communicates directly with the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs: To authenticate your Google account and read spreadsheet data. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Notion API: To create pages, tables, and databases in your Notion workspace. Subject to Notion's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT do:

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT track your browsing activity
  • We do NOT store your spreadsheet data
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT have access to your Google or Notion credentials

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Disconnect Google: You can disconnect your Google account at any time through the extension's settings.
  • Remove Notion Token: You can remove your Notion integration token through the extension's settings.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the extension will remove all locally stored data.
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke the extension's access to your Google account at Google Account Permissions.

Security

We implement security best practices:

  • All communication with Google and Notion APIs uses HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth 2.0 is used for secure Google authentication
  • Your Notion token is stored using Chrome's secure storage API
  • The extension only requests minimum necessary permissions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
